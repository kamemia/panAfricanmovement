Welcome to the East African Business & Tourism Conference

The East African Communities Association is a regional inter-governmental organization comprising of seven partner states: Kenya, Tanzania, Uganda, Rwanda, Burundi, South Sudan and the Democratic Republic of Congo(which joined EAC on 29th March 2022 after signing the treaty of Accession). EAC signed its treaty of Establishment on 30th November 1999 and made its headquarters in Arusha- Tanzania.

East African Business & Tourism Conference 2023

The East African Business & Tourism Conference will take place at Laikipia county in Kenya. It will be hosted by the Pan African Movement Kenya in a bid to boost trade, tourism and promote unity amongst the EAC members. All EAC members are welcome to attend the event.

The East African Business & Tourism Conference 2023

Benefits of attending the East African Business & Tourism Conference 2023 are:

  • It will boost brand visibility by increasing awareness of each countries' businesses and brands.
  • It will help establish the countries' credibility in the industry. This will especially help startups by introducing them to a variety of consumers and potential investors.
  • It will enhance customer loyalty since attendees at the event may include exixting customers of the exhibitors.
  • It will help businesses to reach new target groups, cooperation partners and get instant, direct feedback from customers.
  • Having in-person interactions with the customers can build relations with them and help them understand the exhibitors' businesses.

Laikipia County

Laikipia county lies between the Equator with Latitude 0° 18° South and 0° 51° North and between Longitude 36° 11° West and 37° 24° East. It is a cosmopolitan county and has Nanyuki as the capital town.

  • It has a cool temperate climate throughout the year which supports economic activities
  • It borders Samburu to the North, Isiolo to the North-East, Meru to the East, Nyeri to South East, Nyandarua to the South, Nakuru to the South West and Baringo to the West

Why Laikipia county?

Laikipia is a suitable location because:

  • It is centrally located- it borders seven counties providing it with easy accessibility and good transport links
  • It has adequate accomodation to cater for the guest with its several hotels.
  • It has an attractive leisure environment that offers a degree of enjoyment in addition to taking care of businesses.
  • There is affordable land rates and accomodation charges.
